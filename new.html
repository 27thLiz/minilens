Frequently Asked Questions

Why Godot?

Because it is the best open-source game engine available.

What is that open-source stuff all about?

Open-source means that you have full acsess to the source code, and can remix it as you want (sometimes there are little limitations about releasing it). When you have that access, then you can check it in order to see if there are backdoors, bugs, or other security issues. That means better security, and it usually comes at a lower price. You can learn more about open-source at FSF
