Welcome to Minilens.

1000 years after post-apocalyptic Earth, many form of lifes went extinct, including humans. An alien robot series, called Minilens, are cleaning up earth and collecting surviving flora.

How to make levels

Create a directory at levels/my_pack , rename my_pack as you wish.
At levels directory, there is a level_blank.xml copy it at my_pack open it with Godot Engine.
All names should be level_1.xml , level_2.xml etc.
